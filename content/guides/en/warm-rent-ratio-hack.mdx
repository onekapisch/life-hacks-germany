## Practical benchmark workflow

Start with your own monthly net income, then set a hard warm-rent ceiling. Use that ceiling as a filter in every portal.

## Fast stress test

If one unexpected bill would break your monthly budget, the apartment is likely too tight even if the cold rent looked attractive.
